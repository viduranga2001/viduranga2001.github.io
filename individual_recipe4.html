Dhal curry Recipe

Indulge in the delightful taste of Sri Lankan Dhal curry.

Ingredients

  • cup red lentils (dhal)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, sliced
  • 1 cinnamon stick 4 cardamom pods 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 tablespoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds

Instructions

  1. Rinse red lentils thoroughly and set aside.
  2. In a pot, heat oil and add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, cinnamon, cardamom, and curry leaves. Allow them to splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions, garlic, and ginger. Saut√© until onions are golden brown.
  4. Stir in turmeric powder and chili powder. Cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. SAdd diced tomatoes and cook until they soften. Mix in red lentils and coat them in the spice mixture. Pour in enough water to cover the lentils and bring to a simmer. Cook until lentils are tender. Adjust salt according to taste. Serve hot as a side dish with rice or flatbread.

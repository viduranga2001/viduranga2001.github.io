Coconut Sambal Recipe

Enhance your meal with the vibrant flavors of Sri Lankan Coconut Sambal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup freshly grated coconut
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine grated coconut, chopped red onion, green chilies, and chili flakes.
  2. Add lime juice and salt. Mix well.
  3. Allow the sambal to sit for a while to let the flavors meld.
  4. Serve as a side dish with rice or flatbread.

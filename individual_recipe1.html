Pork Curry Recipe

Experience the delicious flavors of Sri Lankan Pork Curry.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, sliced
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons Sri Lankan curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Salt to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, green chilies, cinnamon, cardamom, and curry leaves. Sauté until fragrant.
  3. Stir in Sri Lankan curry powder and turmeric powder. Cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add pork pieces and brown them on all sides.
  5. Mix in diced tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  6. Mix in diced tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  7. Adjust salt according to taste. Serve hot with rice or roti..
